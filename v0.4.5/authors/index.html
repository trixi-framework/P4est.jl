Authors

The development of P4est.jl is coordinated by a group of principal developers, who are also its main contributors and who can be contacted in case of questions about P4est.jl. In addition, there are contributors who have provided substantial additions or modifications. Together, these two groups form "The P4est.jl Authors" as mentioned in the License file.
